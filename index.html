The Process

How It Works

Each engagement begins with a private inquiry and proceeds at the client's pace.

01

Private Request

Clients submit a watch request or inquiry through our concierge system. Each submission is reviewed directly to understand the client's objectives, preferences, and requirements.

02

Sourcing & Evaluation

We assess the request against our current network and identify available pieces or sourcing opportunities. Relevant options are evaluated for condition, provenance, and alignment with the client's criteria.

03

Presentation

Curated findings are presented directly to the client. We provide context on each option — including relevant market considerations — so the client can make an informed decision.

04

Acquisition or Advisory

Depending on the engagement, we facilitate the transaction or provide strategic guidance tailored to the client's objectives. Support remains available throughout the process and beyond when needed.

Our Approach

Our Advisory Approach

Every client engagement is shaped by individual objectives, informed by market knowledge, and conducted with discretion.

Strategic Acquisition

We help clients identify and evaluate acquisition opportunities aligned with their objectives, preferences, and collection criteria. Each opportunity is assessed on its own terms — condition, provenance, market context, and fit — before being presented for consideration.

Collection Development

For clients building or refining a collection over time, we provide perspective on how individual acquisitions relate to broader collection goals. Our guidance is oriented toward long-term coherence and quality rather than volume.

Market Perspective

The luxury watch market is shaped by collector demand, brand heritage, reference scarcity, and shifting preferences. We share informed perspective on relevant market conditions to help clients make considered decisions — without projections or guarantees.

Discreet Transactions

Acquisitions and private sales are approached with discretion, confidentiality, and professionalism. We work through trusted relationships and established industry connections to facilitate transactions that meet client standards on both sides.
