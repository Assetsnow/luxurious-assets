The Firm

Built Around Relationships for Collectors and Owners

Luxurious Assets Consulting LLC is an independent luxury asset firm specializing in watch acquisition, private sales, sourcing, brokering, and advisory services for collectors and enthusiasts seeking exceptional timepieces.

Who We Are

Who We Are

Luxurious Assets Consulting LLC is an independent firm operating at the intersection of watch collecting, private acquisition, and advisory services. We work with collectors, enthusiasts, and owners seeking access to exceptional timepieces — and with those looking to sell or transition pieces from their collections.

Our network spans private collectors, trusted industry relationships, and established sourcing channels. This provides access to highly sought-after timepieces and opportunities not typically available through conventional retail or auction channels.

Every engagement is handled individually. We do not operate as a marketplace, a publication, or a retail platform. We are an active participant in the transactions and relationships that define serious watch collecting.

How We Work

How We Work

Every client relationship begins with a private inquiry and proceeds at the client's pace.

Private and Relationship-Driven

We work through a network of trusted relationships built over time. Access to the pieces our clients seek — and to the right buyers for the pieces they are selling — comes through those relationships, not through a public listing platform.

Discreet by Design

Discretion is not a feature we offer — it is the foundation of how we operate. Inquiries, submissions, transactions, and client relationships are handled with confidentiality and professionalism at every stage.

Direct Communication

Clients communicate directly with our team throughout an engagement. There are no automated systems managing your inquiry. Each submission is reviewed individually and responses reflect a genuine assessment of the situation.

Flexible Engagement

We work across acquisitions, private sales, brokered transactions, sourcing requests, and collection advisory — adapting to the nature of each engagement rather than applying a fixed process to every client relationship.

What Clients Value

What Clients Value

Collectors who work with us consistently return to three things: judgment, transparency, and perspective.

Judgment — the ability to assess a specific piece, a sourcing opportunity, or a collection decision on its own terms rather than applying a generic framework. Transparency — clear, honest communication throughout an engagement, including when the answer is that something is not the right fit. Perspective — an informed view of the market, the reference, and the collection context that helps clients make considered decisions over time.

We are not a transactional service. Our objective is to be useful to serious collectors over the long term — through individual engagements that reflect genuine knowledge and a commitment to the client's interests.
